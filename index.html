Founders

SLN Murthy
CEO

SLN Murthy was the pioneer behind India's very first CAD-based center for PCBs and played a crucial role in the country's defense and missile program. After spending more than a decade at Bharat Electronics, he took on the challenge of a startup with an entrepreneurial spirit. This journey led him to establish a successful startup in the semiconductor and system design domains which was acquired by a European conglomerate. With over 50 years of experience in the industry, SLN Murthy's passion is to develop new generation professionals and trained over 4000 engineers since 2000 in building the trained resource pool for multi nationals and defense R&D groups. He has conducted over 150 training programs to Space, Defense and Telecom, Automotive sectors on SI and PI. He has been a founding member of IEEE-EPS, Technical Advisor –IPC, Senior Member IEEE and has been on committees of many international and National conferences and workshops. Currently, SLN Murthy is Co-Founder and VP of a product company with 3 patents pending. He's also actively involved in guest lecturing at universities, sharing his knowledge about Signal Integrity and educating young minds.

Suresh Subramanyam
CTO

Suresh’s dream to establish Semiconductor Packaging Development capabilities in India was realized about 2 decades back. After spending 8 years in Singapore, he returned to India in 2003, starting the first multi-disciplined team at Intel India, hiring engineers from the US and India. For nearly 20 years at Intel India, Suresh led the way, forming, and guiding multiple teams. His efforts laid a strong foundation for Packaging Development expertise across various divisions in India. He played a pivotal role in bringing about more than 25 successful package tape-outs for CPUs, GPUs, and Northbridge/Gfx Chipsets.

Notably, in 2004, he co-founded the IEEE-EPS Bangalore Chapter, showcasing his visionary zeal and commitment to the field. From 2016 to 2018, he served on the Board of Governors Council of the IEEE-Electronics Packaging Society, USA. With broad experience in high-volume manufacturing, packaging technology development, architectural definition, design, assembly tooling, PCB and systems design, and organizational growth, Suresh's vision to lead research and development efforts in collaboration with academia and premier research institutions, globally, began to take shape.

Satisha Naraharimurthy
COO

Satisha Naraharimurthy is an accomplished entrepreneur with 20+ years of experience in varied leadership roles with NichePro Technologies, NetZealous LLC, and several other ventures. His entrepreneurial journey began with Comcast Solutions, focusing on high end IT corporate training and Technical recruitment for several multinational product based captive centre’s. He founded NichePro Technologies, offering IT services and solutions, before its acquisition by TeamLease Services. He leads NetZealous, providing professional training in regulatory compliance, HR, healthcare, and IT and it made pioneer in the North American market.

As an angel investor he supports entrepreneurship by investing into several high end technology companies like Virtulive Technologies, offering innovative collaboration solutions, Cloudcollab Technologies, an niche IT services provider with Infinipack being the latest in his quest for high growth, new age ventures. Satisha's journey reflects his commitment to entrepreneurship and professional growth and he continues to promote, invest and mentor start ups across diverse industries
