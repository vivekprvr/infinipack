Our Story

In 2023, with global semiconductor industry transforming into chip let driven era, Suresh and SLN Murthy founded Infinipack with purpose of building a platform to empower the eco-system with world-class advanced packaging and systems engineering talent.

Suresh

Suresh always dreamed of spearheading the growth of Packaging design in India. After 8 years spending time in Singapore, he returned to India in 2003, determined to turn his dream into reality by developing skills in the specialized field of Semiconductor Packaging. For nearly 20 years at Intel India, Suresh led the way, forming and guiding multiple teams. His efforts laid a strong foundation for Packaging Design expertise across various divisions in India. He played a pivotal role in bringing about more than 25 successful package tape-outs for CPUs, GPUs, and Northbridge/Gfx Chipsets.

Notably, in 2004, he co-founded the IEEE-EPS Bangalore Chapter, showcasing his commitment to the field. From 2016 to 2018, he served on the Board of Governors Council for the IEEE-Electronics Packaging Society, USA. With broad experience in high-volume manufacturing, packaging technology development, architectural definition, design, assembly tooling, and organizational growth, Suresh's vision to lead research and development efforts in collaboration with academia and premier research institutions globally began to take shape.

SLN Murthy

SLN Murthy was the pioneer behind India's very first CAD-based center for PCBs and played a crucial role in the country's defense program. After spending more than a decade at BHEL, he took on the challenge of a startup with an entrepreneurial spirit. This journey led him to establish several successful startups in the semiconductor field. With more than 50 years of experience in the industry, SLN Murthy's passion grew towards helping businesses thrive, guiding successful startups, and contributing to the growing ecosystem. Currently, SLN Murthy sits on the advisory board of a thriving company and serves as the CEO of a couple of other ventures in the semiconductor sector. He's also actively involved in guest lecturing at universities, sharing his knowledge about Signal Integrity and educating young minds.

In 2023, Heterogeneous Integration and Advanced Packaging started to become prominent in the era of Chiplets, it became critical to drive the global semiconductor industry forward. Overall System-level design and engineering was taking precedence over individual performances to enable better user experiences and sustainable designs. Traditionally, package design was housed within OSATs or MNCs, it was then, Suresh and SLN Murthy finally decided to lead the way in advanced semiconductor packaging for India. That's when they established Infinipack. Their goal was simple yet powerful: to create a hub that could nurture top-notch talent in advanced packaging and systems engineering, enriching the entire ecosystem.
